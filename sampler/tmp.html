How to balance your life?

Here, we provide advice on your work, friendship, family and hobbies, so as to help you create the life you want! Just try to give it a try!
work
You will need to pay much attention to your work because you make money through doing a job well. Usually, a to-do list is useful to help you see what you need to do on every single day.
friendship
Get contact to your friends as much as possible. People who have similar interests or aspirations are likely to be friends and you get a lot of meaning from them through interacting with them as well as sharing your joy or sorrow with them. So, treasure them!
family
Your family usually contains your wife, your children or/and your parents. Spend some quality time with them please whenever you have free time. They are the people who support you most when you're down and it's important that you pay them back!
hobbies
Do not forget to take care of your hobbies! A good hobby, such as jogging, painting or fishing, can relieve your pressure from heavy daily work and thus help you find the true self!
